Welcome to Accessible Design Co.

At Accessible Design Co., we are dedicated to creating inclusive digital experiences for everyone. Our mission is to help businesses and organizations make their websites and applications more accessible to people with disabilities. We believe that everyone deserves equal access to information and technology, and we are committed to providing the resources and support needed to achieve that goal.

About Us

Welcome to Accessible Design Co., where we create inclusive digital experiences for everyone! Created by Tybrianna Hall to help others add accessibility features to their websites. With he having an uncle that's deaf and struggled to find websites that catered to his needs is what inspired her to provide resources and support for creating accessible digital content.

Services

We offer a range of services to help you make your digital content more accessible, including:

  • Accessibility Audits: We evaluate your website or application to identify accessibility issues and provide recommendations for improvement.
  • Consultation: We work with you to develop an accessibility strategy and provide guidance on best practices for inclusive design.
  • Training: We offer training sessions to educate your team on accessibility principles and how to implement them effectively.
  • Custom Solutions: We can create custom solutions tailored to your specific needs, such as accessible design templates or assistive technology integration.
